### About
<div class="header_line"></div>

🎯 Generation of a landscape by applying a (statistical) operator on a (discrete scalar) field<br/>
🧠 Inspired by [RandomFractalTerrain-Vpython](https://github.com/ragnraok/RandomFractalTerrain-Vpython)<br/>
🐍 A [VPython demo](https://www.glowscript.org/#/user/zeger.hendrikse/folder/Math/program/FractalTerrain) is available as well, see [fractal_terrain.py](https://github.com/zhendrikse/physics-in-python/blob/main/vpython/fractal_terrain.py), but significantly slower!<br/>
