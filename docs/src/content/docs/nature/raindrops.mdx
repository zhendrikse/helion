### About
<div class="header_line"></div>

🎯 Solving the wave equation using a finite difference method for implementing the Laplace operator/solver<br/>
🧠 Inspired by [this example](https://beltoforion.de/de/unterhaltungsmathematik/2d-wellengleichung.php) from [Recreational Mathematics with Python](https://github.com/beltoforion/recreational_mathematics_with_python)<br/>
🐍 Also available in [VPython](https://vpython.org/) as
[raindrop_waves.py](https://github.com/zhendrikse/physics-in-python/blob/main/vpython/raindrop_waves.py), but significantly slower!<br/>
👉 Wave equation with velocity-based damping:

$$
u_{tt} + \gamma u_t = c^2 \nabla^2 u
$$

Where $u$ is the height of water, $u_t$ the velocity, and $\gamma$ the damping coefficient.
